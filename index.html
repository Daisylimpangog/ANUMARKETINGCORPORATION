HOW IT STARTED

The sole owners are Korean citizens namely Kim Min Geon (Usher) and Choi In Hyuk(Allen). Their English names were used as the name of the company which means, “A” stands for Allen “N” and “U” as Usher. But Mr. Allen is not affiliated to the company anymore. It all started when Mr. Kim had an agreement with Bioskin Laboratories from Davao to export Virgin Coconut Oil to Korea last 2018. After the success, Mr. Kim decided to distribute products here in the Philippines. At first, the company just supplies souvenir products but in the year 2019 he decided to distribute other goods such as cosmetics and delicacies. With the determination of the owner, he registered everything legally to supply his products to Metro Retails Stores Supermarkets, Islands Souvenirs and Globemerchants, Inc. Year 2020, COVID happened and the company shut down. But with resoluteness of Mr. Kim the company reopened last May 2022 with a bigger opportunity in collaboration with the same purchasing companies. And now, with the help of Islands souvenir, we are now distributing nationwide including SM Exchange.
